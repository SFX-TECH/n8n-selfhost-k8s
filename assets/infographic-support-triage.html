AI Support Triage, automated

What a support coordinator does by hand all day, sorting and routing incoming messages, handled in one flow that runs in seconds.

Messages arrive

Support tickets land in the queue: billing problems, frustrated customers, feature ideas, and how-to questions.

▼

AI reads and rates each one

A local AI model tags every message with a topic, an urgency, the customer mood, and the right team, then drafts a first reply.

▼
3

Smart routing

Each ticket takes one of two paths, automatically.

🚨

Urgent or unhappy

High urgency or a negative mood gets escalated to a human right away.

Escalate now
✍️

Everything else

A drafted reply is queued for a quick human review before it goes out.

Reply drafted

4

Logged for the team

Every triaged ticket is saved to a log, with a running summary by urgency and team for the daily standup.
